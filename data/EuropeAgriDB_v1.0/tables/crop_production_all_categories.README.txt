Dataset name
------------

crop_production_all_categories



Description
-----------

Crop production in 17 crop categories.

Harvested areas may add up to more than the total cropland area due to
multicropping or intercropping.

Harvests and yields are given in terms of nitrogen (N) contained in the primary
crop product (e.g., grain N in the case of cereals).



Variables
---------

Symbol: A
Label: Harvested area
Unit: Mha

Symbol: H
Label: Harvested quantity
Unit: Gg N

Symbol: Y
Label: Yield
Unit: kg N / ha
