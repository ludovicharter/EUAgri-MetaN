Dataset name
------------

crop_production_fodder_crops



Description
-----------

Crop production in 6 fodder crops.

Aggregated to categories in dataset "crop_production_all_categories".

Harvests and yields are given in terms of nitrogen (N) contained in
the crop product. In the case of temporary grassland (G1000), the "harvest"
includes also grazing intake.



Variables
---------

Symbol: A
Label: Harvested area
Unit: Mha

Symbol: H
Label: Harvested quantity
Unit: Gg N

Symbol: Y
Label: Yield
Unit: kg N / ha
