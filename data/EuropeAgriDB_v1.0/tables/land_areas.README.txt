Dataset name
------------

land_areas



Description
-----------

Agricultural land areas

- Csum: Sum of harvested crop areas.
- Ctot: Cropland area (total area used for arable crops + permanent crops + fallow)
- C: Cropland in use. C = min(Csum, Ctot).

  If Ctot > Csum, the difference (Ctot - Csum) usually roughly equals fallow.
  Csum sometimes exceeds Ctot, most likely due to multicropping and intercropping.
  In most cases where

- PG: Permanent grassland, defined as grassland lasting more than five years.
- TG: Temporary grassland, including grass-legume mixtures. Temporary grassland is
  part of arable land.

Total agricultural land use = Ctot + PG.
